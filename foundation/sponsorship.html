Corporate Sponsorship

The OpenDDS Foundation relies on the financial support of our Contributing Members to support and grow the project. If you are interested in becoming a Contributing Member or have any questions regarding sponsorship details, please contact us.

We encourage organizations that make use of the OpenDDS project to support the continued evolution and growth of the technology through the OpenDDS Foundation Corporate Sponsor Program. The generous support of our Corporate Sponsors allows the Foundation to continue to ensure innovation and advancement of OpenDDS technology as a leading-edge solution for distributed systems.

Contributing Members may support the Foundation by contributing at one of our defined sponsorship levels or by making an anonymous donation to the OpenDDS Foundation.

You may choose between five sponsorship levels:

  • Ambassador
  • Platinum
  • Gold
  • Silver
  • Bronze

Please reach out to the OpenDDS Foundation Board of Directors at foundation@opendds.org to discuss additional opportunities to support and engage with our team.

Individuals may also support the OpenDDS Foundation through our Community "All Star" Sponsorship Program.
